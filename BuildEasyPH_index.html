Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive my download after payment? +
After payment on Gumroad or Etsy, you will receive an email with a download link immediately. The file is a PDF you can save on your phone, tablet, or computer.
Are the contract templates editable? +
Yes! The contract templates come as PDF with fillable fields. You can also copy the text into Microsoft Word or Google Docs for full editing.
Do the guides apply to all cities in the Philippines? +
Yes — the guides cover national requirements (PD 1096, DPWH) that apply everywhere. We also note where LGUs may have additional local requirements, especially for Metro Manila.
Can I use these for commercial projects? +
The Building Permit Guide and Cost Estimator include notes for both residential and light commercial projects. The contract templates are best suited for residential construction.
